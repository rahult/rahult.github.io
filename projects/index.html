MedeCrush (iPhone App)

MedeCrush is a fast interactive iPhone App that gives health professionals in Australia instant access to the latest information about the correct crushing and dissolving of medications. The information includes alerts for medications that require special handling and medications that may increase the risk of swallowing complications. A list of alternative medications is provided where applicable. (October 2012 – May 2013)

South Australian Strategic Plan

This Plan guides individuals, community organisations, governments and businesses to secure the wellbeing of all South Australians. South Australia’s Strategic Plan contains our community’s visions and goals; its 100 measurable targets reflect our priorities. The Plan identifies priorities for South Australia. (June 2010 – Present)
